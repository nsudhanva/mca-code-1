The Department of MCA was established in 1997. The department has been offering only one program, Master of Computer Applications under Visvesvaraya Technological University.

The employee strength of the department is 35 members with 24 teaching faculty and 11 non teaching staff members. The department has 5 faculty members with PhD degree and 8 members currently pursuing their PhD studies and 3 members registered for PhD. The department has 3 professors, 3 Associate Professors and 18 Assistant Professors. The department also has a research centre recognized by VTU and well facilitated computer labs. The number of publications in the past one year has been twentyfive. The department has initiated TEA (Technical but Eccentric yet Affable) Talks where the faculty of the department and the institution are encouraged to give an impromptu talk about their research finding and open research problems in their specialized domains and this event is happening successfully.

The total number of students currently enrolled in this course is 360. Sufficient class rooms and systems are provided for their use. The department has initiated a FOSS – Free Open Source Software Lab. A server is available for the faculty and the students that is physically located at the CISCO Lab with a configuration of 1x X3430, Intel 3420 Chipset.

The department has 985 books in its department library that have come from Central Library. Approximately 40 books have been donated to the department library from alumni, faculty and students. The department has procured three journals.

For the students the department conducts SASP (Student Academic Support Program), GSDP (Gifted Student Support Program) and TSDP (Total Student Development Program). SASP is conducted in all the semesters for those students whose performance is poor (Test marks < 25 and Attendance less than 85%). GSDP is offered to students excelling in their studies and are supervised by professors to work on research and entrepreneur projects. Students are also encouraged to present their papers in conferences and financially supported.

Pre placement training is being conducted for IV sem by department and college. Internship process is handled at the department level to carry out VI semester dissertation. The students have been given the opportunity to visit industries such as CTS, CISCO. Syllabus oriented Guest Lectures are conducted for the students in every semester as part of their curriculum. A Faculty Advisory Diary is maintained for each student to address student’s grievances, to monitor the academic performance.

The department will conduct 1 day workshop on Python , 7th September 2013 and 2 day National Conference on Data Science, 7th-8th November 2013.

The department organized First International Conference on Multimedia processing, Communication and Computing Applications” on 13-15th Dec 2012.

The department has initiated an environmental awareness program called Paryavaran. It is conducted every year in the department by the students.

At Home function has been organized in the department on 01st June 2013 for 2013 outgoing batch students. Best student award has been given to Sruthi Kadam.

The students of the department have participated in fests held at various colleges in the past one year. They have won around 27 prizes in around 09 events.

Sixth semester students did research work and presented their outcomes in different National level conferences, Many of them have won prizes.

To excel in imparting quality education, create ethically strong, creative, analytical, technically superior, knowledgeable, innovative and inquisitive minds.

Mission

To become dynamic and vigorous knowledge hub with an exposure to state of art computer technologies and empower students to become skilled and ethical entrepreneurs while endorsing free and open source software learning & usage.To promote and adapt professional development in a perpetual demanding environment and nurture Megaminds for Competent Accomplishments.
